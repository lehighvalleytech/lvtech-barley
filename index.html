Events


Lehigh Valley Startup Weekend

Startup Weekends are 54-hour events where developers, designers, marketers, product managers, and startup enthusiasts come together to share ideas, form teams, build products, and launch startups!

The next Lehigh Valley Startup Weekend will be on November 15th to the 17th. For more information go to lehighvalley.startupweekend.org. Follow @LVPASW for updates.

Lehigh Valley Hackathons

Our Hackathons are 2 to 3 day events where engineers, developers, hackers, makers, and technology enthusiasts come together to make something new in the Lehigh Valley.

The next hackathon is the Lehigh Valley National Day of Civic Hacking on June 1st and 2nd at the Bridgeworks Enterprise Center in Allentown, PA. For more information and to REGISTER go to hack.lehighvalleytech.org. Follow @LVTECH for updates.

Amazi makes it easy to find locations—such as businesses, campuses and city water stations—to fill eco-friendly containers. Our goal is to make filling reusable bottles as easy as buying a new one. We are dedicated to ending the consumption of bottled water, by offering a sensible alternative, and aiding in the clean water crisis around the globe.

 

Locally is designed to show you what is happening around you, right now. We share experiences you won't see on Google Maps or Foursquare. It's easy to find a park, restaurant, museum or winery but it is hard to find amazing experiences offered within these venues. Locally solves this problem.

It's simple.

  • Visit locally.fm on your computer or mobile device.
  • See what's in your area.
  • Select an experience from the map or list to learn more about it.
  • Invite friends by sharing on Twitter or Facebook.
  • Go have a great time!
